SCALAR Lab

Our research focuses on scalable compute and memory systems for improving performance, energy efficiency, security, reliability especially when running emerging applications such as DNN model.

Our research ares includes the following applications.

- Security of Deep Neural Networks

- DRAM Research for Secure and Reliable Operations

- Spin Memories for Future Computing Systems

- Error Correction Code Design

- Sparse Matrix-Vector Multiplication on Various Platforms
