SCALAR Lab

    Our research focuses on scalable compute and memory systems for improving performance, energy efficiency, security and reliability on various emerging applications.

Our research area includes the following applications.

  • Security of Deep Neural Networks
  • DRAM Research for Secure and Reliable Operations
  • Spin Memories for Future Computing Systems
  • Error Correction Code Design
  • Sparse Matrix-Vector Multiplication on Various Platforms
